Categories

All beers have an average percentage of alcohol of 5%. Wines have 12% of alcohol and are consumed per 10 cL. Shots are baverages such as rum, vodka, cognac, whiskey and liquors. They have on agerage 35% alcohol in 5 cL glasses. Lastly Cocktails who are all other baverages. They contain 5.6% alcohol in 27.5 cL glasses.

About our app

We wanted to create an app to improve people's awairness considering consuming alcohol and drunk driving. Although we did excessive research on calculating the blood alcohol content (BAC), we cannot guarantee that the determined BAC value is accurate. We are therefore in no way responsible for any action you take upon the information in this app, using it is at your own risk.
